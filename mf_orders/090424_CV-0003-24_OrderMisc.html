IN THE MAYFLOWER DISTRICT COURT

FOR THE DISTRICT OF CLARK COUNTY

BrendaPopplewell,

Plaintiff(s).

v.

98MrFilmFilm,

Defendant(s).

Case No.: CV-0003-24

Before: Hon. Albert Wellesley

ORDER (EXCLUDING MEMORANDUM OPINION)

ORDER DISMISSING PROCEEDINGS IN ACCORDANCE WITH MAYFL. R. CIV. P. 31(a)(2)-(3)

The Court, having noticed on its own motion that the plaintiff in the case referenced above has departed from all communication channels, established for the exchange of information and updates on the case, and from that moment effectively ceased to engage with the Court on its own civil suit, and left no instructions, as the original party to the case, for the Court to rely upon and consequently prevent the Court from considering substitute counsel or any other person, natural of the body or of some other entity sanctioned in law, to assume the role of Plaintiff, and for these reasons the Court sees fit to declare that the plaintiff has abandoned all claims against the defendant,

IT IS ORDERED that the claims of the plaintiffs in the case against 98MrFilmFilm be dismissed without prejudice sua sponte.

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the remaining parties are released from these claims without the award of costs, and that any pending motions and applications to the court are dismissed accordingly, and costs for those pending motions etc. also not be awarded.

In Lander, Mayflower, this 4th day of September, 2024


Chief Judge, Mayflower District Courts
