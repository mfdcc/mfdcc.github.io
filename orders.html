Orders made

All written orders (except for sealed orders or where a written one is not required) made by the judge will be posted here.

The orders on this webpage will be listed as:
Year/Month/Day.CaseName.CaseNumber.OrderType - Title of Order

For example, an order made to dismiss a case would be put in here as:
2024/August/31.MF_v_Jane Doe.CV-0000-24.OrderMTD - Motion to Dismiss the case accepted

If you are looking for a particular order, type in the year or the case number followed by a period then the order type (e.g. "9700000001.OrderMTD"). These are the keys that I will use for "OrderType":

  • OrderMTD - Motion to Dismiss
  • OrderQuash - Quash Order
  • OrderMTA - Motion to Amend
  • OrderSW - Search Warrant (this won't be something I write regularly)
  • OrderAW - Arrest Warrant (this won't be something I write regularly)
  • OrderDisc - Orders to Disclose (Discovery purposes)
  • OrderMisc - Miscellaneous/unspecified order (any other order, the title will specify what it is)
  • Summons

Orders that require the court's seal will be included on the order here. If the order is written and sealed originally in PDF, the PDF version shall prevail.
