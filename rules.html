Rules in the Chamber

Effective from: August 31st, 2024
These rules are effective for all cases docketed to this judge's chamber:

Albert Wellesley

These rules must be observed at all times when engaging with the judge or his/her staff via electronic communication or in-game. Failing to do so may result in a contempt of court charge levied against the perpetrator at the judge's own motion (if it is lawful):
  1. No derogatory remarks may be made against anyone involved in proceedings pending in this chamber, including:
    1. racist rhetoric and commentary;
    2. sexist rhetoric and commentary;
    3. ableism of both physical and mental nature;
    4. mental health including anxiety and depression;
    5. transphobic rhetoric and commentary;
    6. xenophobic rhetoric and commentary;
    7. discriminatory remarks on ethnicity;
    8. any protected characteristic defined in state law or under Roblox or Discord Terms of Service.
  2. No one may engage in verbal or physical altercation with parties involved, members of the Court and any officers of the Court.
  3. No one shall engage in ex parte communication regarding a case pending in this chamber.
  4. No one may post content that is commonly accepted as Not Safe for Work, including:
    1. nudity of someone or themselves and adult content in general which include adult or mature conversations;
    2. gore, which includes the mutilation of an animal or person;
    3. pictures or videos of someone engaging in the use of controlled substances, narcotics or prescription drugs;
    4. the posting of personal information of anyone that is meant to be private (in such cases this will be forwarded to the appropriate authorities outside the scope of the simulation);
    5. visual or text containing content that contravenes rule 1 in these rules or is politically or racially extreme;
    6. all of the above in cartoon or fictional depiction.
  5. No one may discuss any part of the proceedings that is sealed and is pending in this chamber with those not involved in those proceedings.
  6. No one is permitted to promote any rhetoric, symbolism or belief pertaining to national socialism. Such symbolism extends to images, other media visuals, speeches and signs that are both explicit and intentionally obscure or misleading used to represent neo-Nazi values (e.g. '1488' or '88'). Such remarks are reported to moderation in the first instance.
  7. Any form of abuse not mentioned explicitly in these rules are also prohibited, and reprimands will be issued without warning if such abuse is directed towards officers and members of the Court.
N.B. of the following:

Breaking the rules may result in me removing you from the courtroom or channel, or potentially remove you from participating in the case indefinitely (if permitted by law). In such a case your lawyer should continue to liaise and communicate with you about the case unless they withdraw for good reason.

If you break these rules as a lawyer, you may be reprimanded to the State Bar in addition to any of the penalties above.

If your actions are so egregious that it does not only break the rules but may lead to you breaking them again and you show no effort to prevent yourself from breaking them, you may be charged with contempt. You may also be charged with a criminal offense if a crime is committed as well while breaking these rules (such as rule 2).

The venue for which these rules apply are not fixed. They apply to wherever the judge is expected to be present. At the moment, it can be:

  • Lander Courthouse
  • Mersea Town Hall Courthouse
  • Any temporary Courthouse
  • Special courtrooms where the judge is invited to preside
  • Discord servers

Anyone who breaks these rules may first be warned, up to three warns. After that, they may be found in contempt of court given it is appropriate to.

These rules are not in contravention of local, state or UC laws and the State and UC constitution.
